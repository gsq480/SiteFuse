Setup
1) Appearance, Themes, Add New, Upload Theme, select nova-uxgov-90s.zip.
2) Activate. Pages and sample content will be created.
3) Replace the email in theme.js with your address to enable the mailto form or install a forms plugin.
4) Add thumbnails to your Work items for a stronger grid.
